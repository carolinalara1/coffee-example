Coffee Street

It was a thrilling period that allowed us to expand and evolve while remaining committed to our core mission of delivering freshly roasted 100% Arabica coffee, complemented by exceptional service and a distinct personality.

Fueled by our unwavering love for coffee, we've transformed into a coffee destination offering an array of choices, including single-origin coffees, fair-trade organic options, and uniquely flavored blends—all skillfully roasted and infused with flavors right at our location. Our offerings extend beyond coffee to include a wide selection of loose-leaf teas, delectable cocoas, and an assortment of brewing equipment, alongside a delightful assortment of miscellaneous items.

At our Espresso Bar, you'll savor our daily coffee brews, explore specialty beverage creations, indulge in freshly baked treats, and peruse a light yet satisfying lunch menu.
